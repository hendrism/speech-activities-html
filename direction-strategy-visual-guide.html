Direction Strategy Chart

Use this visual to slow down, spot the most important parts of a direction, and choose the best strategy to ace it the first time.

1

Scan & Notice

Think: What is this asking me to do? Circle verbs and time words. If it's spoken, replay it in your head or ask to hear it again.

2

Highlight the Core

Spot the must-do items: Underline steps, people, tools, and deadlines. Chunk long directions into smaller pieces you can handle.

3

Plan Your Strategy

Pick what helps: Will you rehearse, visualize, write it down, or set a reminder? Say the plan out loud so you know the order.
