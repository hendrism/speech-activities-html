High School Social Conflict Reflection

Explore real-world challenges high school students face with friends, teachers, and activities. For each situation, communicate your thoughts and feelings, then decide how you want to respond.

Group Project, Uneven Effort

English • Grade 10 • Deadline Tomorrow
Your English group project presentation is tomorrow. You spent two nights finishing the slides and script, but one teammate, Jayla, said she was “too busy” and hasn’t contributed anything. Now she texts that she expects you to “cover for her” during the presentation.

What are you thinking right now?

Name your thoughts about Jayla and the project.

What are you feeling?

Identify emotions (annoyed, stressed, nervous, etc.).

How will you communicate?

Plan what you want to say when you reply to Jayla.
