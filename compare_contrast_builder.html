Clinician guidance

  • Listen for at least four concrete details across the collected sentences.
  • Monitor grammar: comparative / superlative forms, adjective choice, subject-verb agreement.
  • Highlight sentence variety (similarities, differences, evaluations) and prompt expansions.
  • Decide on immediate vs delayed feedback based on scaffolding level and student regulation.
  • Fade supports gradually by hiding notes, reducing prompts, or switching to spontaneous speech.
