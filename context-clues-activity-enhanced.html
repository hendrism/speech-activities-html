About This Activity

Context clues help you figure out the meaning of unfamiliar words by looking at the words and sentences around them. There are five main types of context clues:

Definition: The meaning is stated directly in the sentence.
Synonym: A similar word is used nearby.
Antonym: An opposite word helps explain the meaning.
Example: Examples of the word are given.
Inference: You use clues from the whole sentence to figure out the meaning.
